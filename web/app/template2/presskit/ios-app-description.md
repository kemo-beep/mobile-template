Clarity is an iOS focus app built for deep work.

If your day gets fragmented by distractions, Clarity helps you reclaim attention with structured focus sessions, smart reminders, and actionable productivity insights.

- **Smart Reminders:** Receive timely nudges that support momentum without interrupting flow.

- **Simple Interface:** Clean, modern design that keeps your attention on what matters.
- **Privacy-First:** Built with user trust in mind from day one.

### Best for

You do not need another complicated productivity system. Clarity gives you a practical structure you can actually stick with.
