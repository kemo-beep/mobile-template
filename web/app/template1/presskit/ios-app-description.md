## 3) Full App Description (App Store)

Lumina is a beautifully minimal iPhone app for mindful journaling and daily habit tracking.

Designed for clarity, Lumina helps you slow down, reflect intentionally, and build better routines without noise or complexity.

### What you can do with Lumina
- **Guided Reflection:** Use gentle prompts to organize thoughts and reduce mental clutter.
- **Habit Tracking:** Log daily habits in seconds with a clean, distraction-free flow.
- **Streak Awareness:** Stay motivated with simple progress indicators and momentum cues.
- **Focused Experience:** Native iOS design built to feel calm, fast, and intuitive.
- **Privacy-First:** Your personal data is treated with care and built around user trust.

### Who it is for
Lumina is ideal for people who want a consistent daily reset: creators, founders, students, and anyone trying to build calmer, healthier routines.

### Why Lumina
Most productivity apps are crowded and overwhelming. Lumina is intentionally simple so you can think clearly, write honestly, and move through your day with purpose.

Download Lumina and start your next day with clarity.
